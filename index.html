Mabbu is the Telugu word for Cloud. Mabbu9 is our version of cloud9, or bliss. We're a cloud studio. We develop exuberant cloud based apps, and specialize in JavaScript, NodeJS, HTML & CSS.

Mabbu9 was an idea we had during our training at MissionRnD. We initially played around with a number of social ideas to go with, and finally cloud-based social apps/games sparked our interest.

We are currently experimenting with the concept of virtual office space. Why does anyone need an explicit big and bulky office when developing apps for the cloud? We feel that the internet has come a long way in connecting each other. Why not use it for collaboration and development? More information will be updated later ;)
